Danna Leon

Psychology

Danna is currently a second year student at the University of California in Riverside studying the field of psychology in hopes of pursuing developmental psychology. After spending most of her life in Mexico she moved to the United States for a better life and has had an interest in psychology ever since she was in grade school.She spends a lot of her time volunteering in animal shelters back home in San Francisco due to the over population of stray animals around her community.

In her free time, she likes to make jewlery for her small business as she is constantly getting new orders to wrap crystals and make rings, necklaces and pendants. It is important to her that her customers are always satisfied so she puts in a lot of work towards her business while at the same time managing school. She also enjoys hiking new trails around her university and exploring new places with her roomates to form new memories.

She hopes to one day be able to open up her very own laboratory or clinic of some sort in San Francisco and conduct studies to help the future of humanity in terms of development. She thouroughly enjoys helping others and it makes her feel accomplished and good about herself. She also hopes to one day build a clinic in her home town in Mexico in order to help the people in need there as there is a strong population of underdeveloped older people as well as an underdeveloped younger population.
